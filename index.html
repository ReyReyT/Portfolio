Half Hearted Lee.

Is the main project I worked on during my time at college. This game was made my friend and I, although the art was done by another member of our course. The idea for our game was to make a puzzle platformer, where the user would play as the main protagonist Lee. The problem is Lee has been cut in half so the aim of the game is to reunite both of Lee's halves. We were going to have multiple ways of doing this towards the end of the game, he could become fully angelic, fully evil or the final option would be to become human again. As the name suggests he was half heartedly trying to reconnect his two halves as one was evil and the other was good, because he was cut in half he had to hop everywhere which was tiring for him, he wanted to reconnect to complete himself again, but he did not want the evil half of himself back so he would complete his mission half heartedly.
